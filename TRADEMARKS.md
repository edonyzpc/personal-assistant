This document records the project trademark and brand boundary for Personal

version 2.8.0. It does not license the following brand assets or market
identity to forks:

- `Personal Assistant`
- the `personal-assistant` plugin ID

- confusingly similar names or visual identity that imply official status

## Fork Requirements

If you distribute a fork or modified version, you must make the fork clearly
distinguishable from the official project:

- rename the plugin
- change the plugin ID
- remove official Personal Assistant icons, logos, and release branding
- state clearly that the fork is unofficial and not endorsed by the maintainer
- preserve required copyright, license, and source-availability notices

You may make nominative references such as "forked from Personal Assistant"
when they are accurate and not misleading.
